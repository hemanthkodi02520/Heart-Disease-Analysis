# ❤️ Heart Disease Analysis Dashboard using Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Heart Disease Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Tableau**. The dashboard analyzes various health-related factors and their relationship with heart disease using a publicly available heart disease dataset.

The objective of this project is to identify patterns, trends, and insights that can help understand the impact of lifestyle, demographic, and medical conditions on heart disease.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze heart disease distribution across different demographic groups.
- Identify the relationship between diabetes and stroke.
- Examine the influence of smoking and alcohol consumption on heart disease.
- Visualize race-wise distribution of heart disease.
- Explore general health, physical activity, BMI, and age-related patterns.
- Build an interactive and responsive Tableau dashboard for data-driven decision making.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

### 1. Gender vs Heart Disease
Displays the distribution of heart disease cases based on gender.

### 2. Diabetic vs Stroke
Shows the relationship between diabetes and stroke among patients.

### 3. Race-wise Heart Disease
Visualizes heart disease distribution across different racial groups.

### 4. Impact of Smoking and Alcohol
Analyzes the combined effect of smoking and alcohol consumption on heart disease.

### 5. General Health vs Heart Disease
Shows heart disease cases based on self-reported general health status.

### 6. Physical Activity vs Heart Disease
Compares heart disease occurrence among physically active and inactive individuals.

### 7. Age vs BMI vs Diabetics
Treemap visualization showing the relationship between age category, diabetic status, and average BMI.

### 8. Other Diseases vs Stroke
Analyzes the association between other medical conditions and stroke.

---

## 🎛️ Interactive Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides interactive filters for:

- Heart Disease
- Stroke
- Diabetic
- Race
- Alcohol Drinking

Users can dynamically explore the data by selecting different filter values.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Heart disease is observed across different age groups and genders.
- Diabetes and stroke show a noticeable association.
- Smoking and alcohol consumption influence heart disease occurrence.
- BMI tends to be higher among diabetic individuals.
- Heart disease is present across all general health categories.
- Physical inactivity may be associated with higher heart disease cases.
